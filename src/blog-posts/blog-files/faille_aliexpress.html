Aliexpress items for cheap

When creating a new account on Aliexpress you have a big discount on the first item you buy, this post is basically a guide on how to create lots of accounts without getting banned.

Getting the email address

When you sign up on Aliexpress you are asked for an email and a password, or something like a google account. Using a google account is safer in the way that you are less likely to get declined when doing a purchase. However, I have found an easy workaround: disposable email services (e.g. mailinator) On these types of services it is possible to create an email address without a password, by just typing it it.
